Pizza Recipe

Homemade pizza that I love to share with friends and family on a Friday night! Cheese...good, pepperoni...good.

This recipe is super easy and only takes about 40 minutes from start to finish! Its fun for the whole family to enjoy and tastes great!

You can cook this dough so many different way, in the oven, on the BBQ, using an Ooni, or any other oven you can think of, the possibilities are endless!

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1 teaspoon yeast
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the toppings:

  • Pizza sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Add the yeast, salt, and flour to a bowl, whisk to combine
  2. Add the water and the oil and stir to combine
  3. Once combined loosley, turn out on a lightly floured surface and knead for 2-5 minutes until smooth
  4. Cover with a towel and let rise for 30 minutes
  5. In the mean time shred your cheese and cut your pepperoni
  6. Preheat the oven to 500F
  7. Once the dough has doubled in size turn out on a lightly floured surface and begin to roll out
  8. Once to your desired thickness add your toppings
  9. Put in oven for 10-12 minutes
  10. Let cool, cut, and enjoy!
