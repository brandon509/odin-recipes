Pasta

This is yummy past becase it has bacon and noodles. How could you not like that!

This is my go to dish and is super easy to get started, only a few simple ingredients and 20 mnuites and you are on your way!

Enjoy this one on your own or with a friend. But remember, you will be fighting over the left overs so watch out

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Panchetta (or bacon if you cant find any)
  • Garlic
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Parm

Steps

  1. Boil water for the pasta
  2. Cook the bacon
  3. Chop your garlic while the bacon is cooking
  4. Drop in the noods
  5. Remove and cut bacon, leave burner on low
  6. Once noods are done, add garlic to bacon grease
  7. Cook garlic until it smells nice and then drop in the pasta, mix to combine
  8. Combine eggs and parm and add to the pasta
  9. Mix and add pasta water if needed
