Grilled Cheese

The best grilled cheese you have ever eaten before in your life!

This is a staple in my house hold. I eat it every weekend becasue it is so simple

I like to experiment with bread and cheese types, open for so much interpretation!

Ingredients

  • Bread of choice
  • Cheese of choice
  • Butter baby!

Steps

  1. Butter one side of each piece of bread
  2. Turn burner on to medium heat
  3. Put one piece of bread butter side down
  4. Shred cheese on to your sandwhich
  5. Top with other poiece of bread
  6. Put on a lid and wait
  7. Flip when butter on top piece has almost melted
